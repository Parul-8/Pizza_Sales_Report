              Pizza Sales Report

              ‎08-10-2023 06:26 PM

              This report performs an exploratory analysis on the pizza sales of the year 2015 of a pizzeria submitted for the semi-annual consideration of the general management based only on the data of the sales orders and product catalog found in the CRM in use in the company.

              The tabular data was exported from the CRM in csv format with a total size of 1.80 MB for a total of over 21,000 sales orders, 32 types of pizza of 5 different sizes giving 96 pizza options for customers with prices ranging from $9.75 to $35.97. No customer information such as name, tax ID number, phone number or email address is present.

              Insights

              1. The day of the week with the highest sales volume is Friday by 12 pm.
              2. The average sales ticket is $38.31
              3. The best-selling pizza is the Chai Chicken size L sold at a price of $20.75.

              Challenges

              • Identify fluctuations in demand throughout the year low demand during certain periods that result in unstable revenues and difficulties in maintaining adequate staffing during peak times.
              • Understand the components that affect customer experience, leading to dissatisfaction and eventually a decrease in sales

              Project Solution

              To solve the management challenges highlighted above we will proceed with descriptive and exploratory data analysis by importing the csv files into Power BI, we will proceed with data cleaning using Power Query by assigning data type to columns, converting the monetary values into the correct format and making the first row of each file as header of the resulting tables.

              We defined a central date table related to the ORDERS table to allow more detailed filters by date. Then the one-to-many relationship was established between the PIZZA and PIZZA TYPES tables, for the ORDER_DETAIL and ORDER tables and finally between ORDER_DETAIL and PIZZA. Below is the figure that demonstrates the aforementioned scheme:

               

               

               

               

               

              In order to convey the key business indicators to general management we have defined 4 cards showing Total Sales Value, Total Sales Orders, Average Sales Ticket and the Quantity of Pizzas Sold. To demonstrate how sales are segmented by pizza category we used the donut chart. The line graphs help to mirror the sales trends according to the time and day of the week and finally we have the bar chart to demonstrate the type of pizza that sells the most and the size of pizza that sells the most.In the top center a month filter is shown to which the dashboard responds and adapts the graphs according to the filter.
